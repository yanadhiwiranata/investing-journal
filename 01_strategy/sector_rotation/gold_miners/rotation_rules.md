## 1. GOLD MINER MACRO REGIME

Gold is primarily a **macro inflation hedge** and **USD inverse play**. Gold miners amplify gold price moves by 1.5x–2.0x.

### Entry Regime Analysis

| Regime | Real Yields | DXY Trend | Gold Price Action | CPI/Inflation | Market Setup | Entry Signal |
|--------|-------------|-----------|-------------------|---------------|--------------|--------------|
| **GOLD BULL** | Falling (>–1.0%) | Weakening | +2–3% weekly | Sticky/higher | Risk-on, inflation fears | ✅ **ACTIVATE** |
| **GOLD SAFE HAVEN** | Rising but Gold +3% | Weak | Breaking to new highs | Geopolitical shock | Flight to safety | ✅ **ACTIVATE** (limited upside) |
| **GOLD STABLE** | Flat ±0.5% | Range-bound | Consolidating $2,250–$2,400 | Moderate 2–3% | Sideways macro | ⏸ **PENDING only** |
| **GOLD HEADWIND** | Rising (>+0.5%) | Strengthening | Lower lows | Disinflation surprise | Risk-off growth | ❌ **AVOID entries** |
| **GOLD COLLAPSE** | Rising sharply | +2%+ move | Breaking support $2,200 | Deflationary shock | Fed pause/pivot narrative | ❌ **EXIT positions** |

**Key Sensitivity:**
- **Real Yields:** Primary driver (–1 bps in 10Y real yield = +2–3% gold move)
- **DXY:** Inverse relationship (DXY –2% = +3–4% gold move)
- **CPI:** Market expectations matter more than prints (if CPI misses disinflationary, gold sells)
- **Fed Pivot Narrative:** Gold highly sensitive to rate-cut expectations; pause/cut expected = gold bullish

**Gold Miner Multiplier:** 
- Gold +2% → Gold miner (CDE, WPM) typically +3–4%
- Gold –2% → Gold miner typically –4–5% (downside leverage is asymmetric)

## 2. PRE-ENTRY MACRO VERIFICATION (MANDATORY)

**Before placing Layer 1 order, verify ALL of these (10-minute process):**

### Checklist
- [ ] **Real Yields:** Is 10Y real yield below +0.5%? (Falling is better than flat)
  - Source: FRED (DFF10) or TradingView
  - If real yield >+0.5% AND rising, **SKIP entry** (headwind building)

- [ ] **DXY:** Is DXY stable or falling? (Not rallying sharply)
  - Source: TradingView, Investing.com
  - If DXY +1% overnight AND gold down, **SKIP** (regime shift)

- [ ] **Gold Price:** Is gold above key support ($4,450–$4,500)?
  - Source: TradingView, FRED commodity data
  - If gold below $4,450, **PENDING ONLY** (wait for flush bottom or consolidation)

- [ ] **CPI/Inflation Expectations:** Next CPI/PPI release bullish or bearish?
  - Source: Economic Calendar (Trading Economics, Investing.com)
  - If market expects lower inflation print, gold may sell anticipation → **WAIT**
  - If market expects higher inflation, gold likely to rally → **GO**

- [ ] **Fed Speakers This Week:** Any FOMC members speaking? What's the tone?
  - Dovish (rate cuts possible) = Bullish gold
  - Hawkish (hold longer) = Bearish gold → **DEFER entry**

- [ ] **Analyst Consensus:** TipRanks PT and recent rating changes
  - Pull 5 latest gold miner ratings: **AU, NEM, CDE** (preferred list)
  - If consensus downgrading, **SKIP** (momentum reversing)

✅ **All checks bullish:** Place Layer 1  
⚠️ **Mixed signals (2+ yellow flags):** PENDING ONLY (lower layers, wait for clarity)  
❌ **More than 2 red flags:** SKIP (wait for macro reset)

## 3. LAYER-BASED ENTRY SYSTEM FOR GOLD MINERS

### Layer 1: Foundation Entry (Patient Accumulation)

**Price Calculation: Analyst Consensus Model**

```
Layer 1 Target = (Average PT from 5 latest analysts) × (0.92 to 0.95)

Example:
- Analyst consensus PT: $60
- Layer 1 target: $60 × 0.93 = $55.80 (7% discount)
```

**Entry Rules:**

| Parameter | Rule | Rationale |
|-----------|------|-----------|
| **Size** | 100 shares | Standard tier, visible in order flow |
| **Order Type** | Limit order, resting | Never chase; patience = discipline |
| **Duration** | 10–14 days | Extended window; gold miners move slow in accumulation |
| **Fill Confidence** | 30–50% expected fill rate | Large discount; not all will fill |
| **Standing Sell** | Place immediately at base case PT | Locks profit, removes emotion |
| **Invalidation** | Cancel if DXY +2% overnight OR gold breaks below $2,200 | Macro override |

**Standing Sell Order (Automatic Exit at Profit):**

```
50% of position exits at: Base Case Price Target + 12–15%
Reason: Locks profit automatically; removes temptation to overstay
Example: Layer 1 $55 + 14% = $62.70 standing sell
```

### Layer 2: Accumulation Build (Confirmation)

**Entry Trigger:** Layer 1 fills + wait 2–3 trading days for price stability

**Entry Rules:**

| Parameter | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| **Timing** | 2–3 days AFTER Layer 1 fill (let volatility settle) |
| **Size** | 100–150 shares (scaled to conviction) |
| **Price** | Layer 1 fill – 3–4% (tighter discount than Layer 1) |
| **Duration** | 5–7 days (more flexible; confirms accumulation trend) |
| **Macro Gate** | Reconfirm: DXY stable/weak, real yields not spiking, gold >$2,250 |
| **Skip If** | Stock drops >5% from Layer 1 fill (suggests thesis failure) |

**Layer 2 Logic:**
- Layer 1 filled at $55 → Layer 2 target: $53 (3.6% lower)
- Layer 2 is scaled down in size because Layer 1 provided base
- Larger discounts create compounding fills; Layer 2 is closer because thesis is now validated

### Layer 3: Core Position (Thesis Validation)

**Entry Trigger:** Layer 2 fills + most recent quarterly earnings confirm thesis

**Entry Rules:**

| Parameter | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| **Timing** | After Layer 2 fill + MUST INCLUDE most recent earnings |
| **Size** | 150–250 shares (largest position; highest conviction) |
| **Price** | Layer 2 fill – 5–6% (larger discount; final accumulation) |
| **Duration** | 10–14 days (patient, final call) |
| **Earnings Gate** | ✅ Revenue tracked to guidance, ✅ AISC on-track, ✅ Production targets met |
| **Macro Gate** | DXY weak, real yields <+0.5%, gold >$2,300 |
| **Skip If** | Earnings miss or guidance cut (thesis broken) OR macro regime to "HEADWIND" |

**Layer 3 Validation Checklist:**
- [ ] Latest quarterly earnings revenue vs. guidance: Within 2% of target?
- [ ] AISC (all-in sustaining cost): On-track? Improving YoY?
- [ ] Production guidance: On-track? Any downward revisions?
- [ ] Cash balance: Healthy? No liquidity concerns?
- [ ] Dividend/buyback: Being maintained? (Indicates confidence)

**Total Position After 3 Layers:**
```
Layer 1: 100 @ $55 = $5,500
Layer 2: 125 @ $53 = $6,625
Layer 3: 200 @ $50 = $10,000
───────────────────────────
Total:   425 shares @ $53.06 avg cost
```

## 4. EXIT RULES FOR GOLD MINERS

### Exit 1: Base Case Profit-Taking (Automatic)

**Trigger:** Standing sell order executes at base case price target

**Rule:** Exit **50% of total position** at base case PT.

```
Example:
Position: 425 shares at avg $53
Base Case PT: $62
Standing Sell: 212 shares at $62 (50% + rounding)
Profit: 212 × ($62 – $53) = +$1,908 locked in

Remaining: 213 shares held for bull case
```

**Why 50%?** 
- Locks in the core expected profit
- Allows hold for upside without risking entire position
- Removes emotional pressure to chase

---

### Exit 2: Bull Case Trim (Opportunistic)

**Trigger:** Stock trades above bull case PT OR major earnings beat OR geopolitical premium fades

**Rule:** Exit **remaining 50%** (or scale out 25% at a time if uncertainty)

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Stock hits $72 (bull case), earnings confirmed, macro intact** | Exit 50% (remaining position); realize $19/share × 213 = +$4,047 |
| **Stock hits $72 but gold suddenly selling off (DXY +2%)** | Exit 75%; keep 25% for lottery (downside now capped) |
| **Stock $68–$71 and you see other sector heating up** | Exit 50%; rotate capital to next sector |

**DO NOT:** Hold through bull case PT if macro is deteriorating. Gold can reverse fast when real yields spike.

---

### Exit 3: Thesis Invalidation (Stop Loss)

**Trigger:** Fundamental breakdown in execution or macro regime shift

| Invalidation Event | Action | Urgency |
|-------------------|--------|---------|
| **Guidance cut >8–10%** | Exit 100% at market | IMMEDIATE (before cascade selling) |
| **Missed production target** | Exit 50%, raise stop to breakeven | URGENT (wait for 1–2 days to see if stock stabilizes) |
| **AISC +20% YoY** | Exit 50%, trim remainder on strength | MEDIUM (cost structure breaking down) |
| **Real yields spike >+1.0% (sharp)** | Exit 50%, move stop to Layer 1 entry | URGENT (macro regime breaking) |
| **Gold breaks below $4,400 sharply** | Exit 25%, raise stops on remainder | URGENT (support broken; leverage working against you) |
| **Stock breaks below Layer 1 entry** | Exit 100% | NO QUESTIONS (position failed) |

**Stop-Loss Price Levels:**
- Layer 1 solo: Stop at $49.50 (–10% from $55 entry)
- Layers 1+2: Stop at $48.00 (–9.5% from $53.06 avg)
- Layers 1+2+3: Stop at $46.00 (–13% from $53.06 avg) — wider because conviction is higher

### Exit 4: Sector Rotation (Opportunity Cost)

**Trigger:** Position at +15–20% AND another sector (oil, tech) heating up sharply

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Gold miner +18%, oil sector rallying +12% this week** | Trim 50% of gold miner; deploy to oil Layer 1 |
| **Gold miner +22%, other sectors flat** | Hold; gold miner is leading rotation |

**Rule:** If you're making good profit in one sector but missing a hotter sector, take 50% profits and rotate. Never FOMO chase other sectors when you're up 20%+.

## 5. GOLD MINER SPECIFIC RISK FACTORS

### Production & Cost Risks
- **AISC (All-In Sustaining Cost):** Monitor quarterly. If climbing >5% YoY, margin compression coming. Exit at +10–15%.
- **Guidance Changes:** Any downward revision = thesis broken. Exit 50% immediately.
- **Mine Depletion:** Long-life assets matter; prefer companies with 10+ year reserves.

### Macro & Commodity Risks
- **Real Yields:** Most sensitive metric. If 10Y real yield breaks above +1.0%, gold miners correct hard (–5–8%).
- **Gold Price Support:** Below $2,200 is danger zone. If gold breaks here, exit positions (don't hold through).
- **DXY:** Strength (>104) pressures gold/gold miners. Weakness (<102) supports them.

### Geopolitical & Central Bank Risks
- **Fed Decisions:** Rate hikes = headwind. Cuts/pauses = tailwind. Check FOMC calendar before entry.
- **Geopolitical Events:** Supply shock (Iran, mining accidents) can spike gold sharply; miners amplify gains.
- **Central Bank Buying:** BOJ, ECB large gold purchases = multi-quarter tailwind; monitor quarterly.

## 10. DISCIPLINE RULES

1. **Macro is primary.** No matter how good the company, if real yields spiking and DXY rallying, reduce position size.
2. **Real yields are the #1 driver.** This is not opinion; it's the empirical relationship. If 10Y real yield breaks +0.5%, gold miners struggle.
3. **No price chasing.** If Layer 1 target $26.50 and stock jumps to $28, WAIT for pullback. Never chase.
4. **Standing sells are non-negotiable.** Place them immediately; they take emotion out of profit-taking.
5. **Earnings must validate thesis.** Before Layer 3, verify latest quarter shows execution.
6. **Stop loss is a hard stop.** If stock breaks below Layer 1 entry, exit 100%. No "hopium."
7. **Gold price matters more than company.** A great company with gold at $2,100 is a bad entry. Wait for gold to stabilize.
