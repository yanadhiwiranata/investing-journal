# AI Compute Infrastructure Research Checklist

Use this checklist for AI semis, foundry, hyperscale cloud, and GPU cloud names.

## 1. Classify The Name First

- Is it a chip designer, foundry, hyperscaler, or GPU cloud operator?
- Is the company a platform leader, share-gain story, or execution-heavy buildout story?
- Which KPI is most important for this name right now?

## 2. Demand Quality

- Is demand driven by training, inference, enterprise software, or cloud capacity resale?
- Are customers expanding production deployments or still testing?
- Is growth broad-based or concentrated in a few customers?

## 3. Supply And Capacity

- For semis: node access, packaging, HBM, networking, and system availability
- For cloud names: power secured, power activated, racks deployed, utilization
- For hyperscalers: capex growth and infrastructure availability

## 4. Economics

- Revenue growth rate
- Gross margin trend
- Operating leverage
- Free cash flow conversion
- Return on capex

## 5. Competitive Position

- Software moat or ecosystem lock-in
- Pricing power
- Switching costs
- Exposure to custom silicon or in-house alternatives

## 6. Balance Sheet And Financing

- Net cash or net debt
- Convertible debt or lease dependence
- Need for repeated capital raises
- Customer concentration and counterparty risk

## 7. Policy And Geopolitics

- Export controls
- China or Taiwan exposure
- Industrial policy support
- Regulatory concentration risk

## 8. Management Credibility

- Did management guide conservatively or over-promise?
- Are major buildout milestones actually being hit?
- Is growth quality improving or being bought with margin sacrifice?

## 9. Final Judgment

- What single KPI matters most next quarter?
- Is this a quality compounder, a share-gain name, or a speculative infrastructure buildout?
- What would invalidate the thesis quickly?
- Does the current valuation already price in perfect execution?
