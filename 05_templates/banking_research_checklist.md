## Step 10: Macro & Regulatory Environment

### US Banks
- [ ] **Fed rate cycle:** Hiking / Holding / Cutting — which phase are we in?
- [ ] **Yield curve shape:** Normal / flat / inverted — NIM implications
- [ ] **Credit cycle:** Expansion / normalization / contraction — NPL trajectory
- [ ] **Basel III endgame / Basel IV:** Is the bank subject to revised capital rules? Capital ratio impact?
- [ ] **DFAST / CCAR:** Upcoming stress test; any constraint on capital return?
- [ ] **CRE / Office Exposure:** Any concentration in commercial real estate (office) — current major risk for US regionals
- [ ] **Deposit competition:** Are money market funds pulling CASA deposits? Deposit beta tracking?

### Indonesian Banks
- [ ] **BI Rate cycle:** Current stance — hiking / holding / cutting
- [ ] **IDR trend:** USD/IDR level; currency weakness raises import inflation and FCY loan stress
- [ ] **OJK macro-prudential policy:** LTV ratio changes for property; GWM adjustments; countercyclical buffer status
- [ ] **Government programs:** KUR (Kredit Usaha Rakyat) — subsidized UMKM loan targets; bank mandated participation
- [ ] **PSAK 71 implementation:** IFRS 9-equivalent in Indonesia; expected credit loss provisioning maturity
- [ ] **Dividend tax treaty:** Relevant for foreign investors holding BBCA, BBRI, BMRI on IDX
- [ ] **MSCI Emerging Markets inclusion / rebalancing:** IDX weight changes affecting institutional flows
