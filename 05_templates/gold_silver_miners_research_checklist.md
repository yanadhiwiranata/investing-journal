# Gold & Silver Miners Research Checklist

Use this checklist when analyzing gold and silver miners.

## Production

- Silver ounces
- Gold ounces
- Silver-equivalent ounces
- QoQ and YoY volume trends
- Mine-by-mine contribution

## Costs

- Cash cost
- AISC
- Cost bridge versus prior quarter
- Whether costs are temporarily elevated or structurally worsening

## Asset Quality

- Grade trend
- Throughput trend
- Recovery rates
- Reserve life
- Development pipeline quality

## Balance Sheet

- Cash
- Debt
- Liquidity
- Near-term funding needs
- Project build capex exposure

## Guidance

- Production guidance
- Cost guidance
- Capex guidance
- Any changes to project timing

## Sector-Specific Risks

- Jurisdiction and permitting
- Community or labor issues
- Royalty / tax regime changes
- Metal price sensitivity

## Final Judgment

- Is the miner growing production?
- Are unit costs moving toward or away from the thesis?
- Is the balance sheet strong enough for the next build cycle?
- What single metric matters most next quarter?
