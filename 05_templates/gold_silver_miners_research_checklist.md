# Gold & Silver Miners Research Checklist

Use this checklist when analyzing gold and silver miners. Organize by priority tier for efficient quarterly reviews.

---

## TIER 1: Critical Metrics (Check Every Quarter)

### Production Trajectory
- [ ] Gold oz produced (QoQ & YoY trend)
- [ ] Silver oz produced (QoQ & YoY trend)
- [ ] Production guidance vs. actual (achievement rate)
- [ ] Mine-by-mine production breakdown
  - [ ] Which asset is driving growth or decline?
  - [ ] Any operational disruptions or recoveries?
- [ ] YoY production growth: **Benchmark** >3% for majors, >5% for juniors
- [ ] Production consistency: **Benchmark** beat or match guidance >80% of time

### Unit Costs (AISC or Cash Cost)
- [ ] AISC per ounce (QoQ & YoY)
- [ ] Cash cost per ounce (QoQ & YoY)
- [ ] Cost bridge: what drove QoQ/YoY change? (labor, energy, throughput, depreciation)
- [ ] Fixed vs. variable cost split estimate
- [ ] Mine-by-mine AISC (identify high vs. low-cost assets)
- [ ] AISC stability: **Benchmark** stable or declining; <$1,200/oz for majors; <$1,400/oz for juniors
- [ ] Cost inflation magnitude: temporary (<5% seasonal) or structural (>10% annual)?

### Grade & Throughput Quality
- [ ] Average ore grade (g/t Au, g/t Ag)
- [ ] Daily mill throughput (tpd) vs. design capacity
- [ ] Recovery rates (% of ore content recovered)
- [ ] Grade trend: improving, stable, or declining?
- [ ] Throughput trend: approaching design capacity or pulling back?
- [ ] Grade benchmark: **Benchmark** stable or improving; declining = future AISC risk
- [ ] Throughput efficiency: **Benchmark** >95% of design capacity

### Reserve & Mine Life
- [ ] Total proven + probable reserves (ounces)
- [ ] Mine life at current production rate (years)
- [ ] Reserve replacement rate (% of current production annually)
- [ ] Any reserve downgrade or impairment?
- [ ] Exploration upside or expansion potential at existing assets?
- [ ] Mine life benchmark: **Benchmark** >10 years for majors; >15 years preferred
- [ ] Reserve replacement benchmark: **Benchmark** ≥100% annually; <80% = red flag

---

## TIER 2: Financial Health (Check Every Quarter)

### Balance Sheet & Liquidity
- [ ] Cash on hand (absolute amount & in days of operating expense)
- [ ] Debt outstanding and maturity schedule
- [ ] Undrawn credit facilities and covenant status
- [ ] Net cash / net debt position
- [ ] Annual capex burn rate (operating + project capex)
- [ ] Project funding needs (next 12–24 months)
- [ ] Liquidity cushion: **Benchmark** Cash >3 months operating expense
- [ ] Debt leverage: **Benchmark** debt/EBITDA <2.0x for majors; <1.5x for juniors

### Cash Generation
- [ ] Operating cash flow (OCF) vs. prior quarter
- [ ] Free cash flow (FCF) = OCF − capex (is company self-funding?)
- [ ] Dividend coverage (FCF / dividend paid), if applicable
- [ ] Share buyback activity (volume, price paid, total $ authorized)
- [ ] OCF trend: improving, stable, or declining?
- [ ] FCF benchmark: **Benchmark** Positive FCF; dividend payout <50% of FCF
- [ ] Capital allocation priority: growth capex vs. shareholder returns vs. deleveraging?

---

## TIER 3: Growth & Execution (Check 2x Annually or Quarterly for Major Projects)

### Development Pipeline
- [ ] Major projects in feasibility or permitting phase (list by name)
- [ ] Expected production start date for each project
- [ ] Capex budget and any overruns / revisions vs. original estimate
- [ ] Permitting status: on track / delayed / at risk
- [ ] Community opposition or indigenous consultation blockers?
- [ ] Funding strategy for project (internal cash, capex, streaming, JV)
- [ ] Execution confidence: on-time, on-budget, on-spec?
- [ ] Project execution benchmark: **Benchmark** On-time, on-budget delivery; permits advancing

### Guidance & Outlook
- [ ] Full-year production guidance (updated? raised/lowered? by how much?)
- [ ] AISC guidance (updated? trend direction?)
- [ ] Capex guidance for current year & forward outlook (2-year view)
- [ ] Project timeline updates (any delays or accelerations?)
- [ ] Management tone on confidence (positive / cautious / concerned)
- [ ] Analyst consensus vs. company guidance (bullish / neutral / bearish spread?)
- [ ] Guidance track record: beat, miss, or match historical average?
- [ ] Guidance benchmark: **Benchmark** Guidance met or slightly beaten; no surprise cuts

---

## TIER 4: Risk & External Factors (Check Quarterly + Ad Hoc)

### Jurisdiction & Regulatory
- [ ] Primary operating jurisdictions (list country, risk tier: low/medium/high)
- [ ] Recent permitting delays, tax increases, or regulatory changes?
- [ ] Indigenous community consultation status (any new concerns?)
- [ ] Labor disputes, strikes, or union activity (active or threatened?)
- [ ] Royalty & tax regime: stable or under pressure for increase?
- [ ] Currency exposure (CAD, AUD, other): [% of costs, hedging]

### Commodity Exposure & Hedging
- [ ] Gold vs. Silver weighting in revenue (% mix)
- [ ] Active price hedges? (% of production hedged, duration)
- [ ] Exposure to other metals (copper, zinc) if applicable
- [ ] Metal price assumptions vs. current spot (gold, silver)
- [ ] Price sensitivity: $100/oz change = $X per share impact
- [ ] Hedge benchmark: **Benchmark** <30% of production hedged; minimal tail risk

### Streaming Agreements & Royalties
- [ ] Total royalty burden (% of revenue)
- [ ] Streaming agreement terms and payments
- [ ] Impact on FCF and net cash generation (% of cash flow)
- [ ] Any refinancing risk or accelerated repayment clauses?
- [ ] Optionality: any streaming deals in market or under discussion?

---

## Final Judgment (After Each Earnings)

- [ ] **Growth Status:** On track / Ahead / Behind guidance
  - Evidence: [production vs. forecast, revised full-year outlook]

- [ ] **Cost Status:** AISC improving / Stable / Deteriorating
  - Evidence: [QoQ/YoY trend, cost bridge, guidance change]

- [ ] **Balance Sheet:** Strong enough for planned capex? Yes / Marginal / Needs capital raise
  - Evidence: [cash position, FCF, debt maturity, project funding]

- [ ] **Thesis Validation:** Bull case strengthened / Unchanged / Weakened
  - Evidence: [execution vs. thesis, guidance change, grade/cost momentum]

- [ ] **Next Critical Data:** What single metric or event matters most next quarter?
  - Answer: [e.g., "quarterly production beat rate", "AISC guidance revisions", "project approval"]

- [ ] **Recommended Action:** Buy / Hold / Reduce / Sell
  - Rationale: [conviction and time horizon]

---

## Analyst-Specific Priority Weight (Customize as Needed)

**Most Important (80% of conviction):**
- Production trend (YoY growth consistency)
- AISC trajectory (cost control vs. inflation)

**Secondary (15% of conviction):**
- Grade sustainability & reserve replacement
- Free cash flow and balance sheet strength

**Tertiary (5% of conviction):**
- Dividend coverage
- Analyst sentiment shifts
