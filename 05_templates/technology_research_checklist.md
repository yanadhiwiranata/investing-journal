# Technology Research Checklist

Use this checklist when analyzing technology companies.

## Revenue Quality

- Revenue growth
- Organic versus acquired growth
- Geographic mix
- Segment mix
- Recurring revenue share

## Profitability

- Gross margin
- Operating margin
- EPS quality
- SBC trend
- Cash conversion

## Demand Signals

- Guidance versus consensus
- Backlog / bookings / RPO
- Customer count or seat growth
- Average spend per customer
- Churn or retention where available

## Strategy

- New product cycle
- AI exposure
- Cloud or infrastructure cycle
- Competitive moat changes

## Risks

- Customer concentration
- Macro slowdown
- Pricing pressure
- Hardware inventory correction
- Regulatory issues

## Final Judgment

- Is growth durable or pulled forward?
- Are margins expanding for the right reasons?
- Is management guiding conservatively or losing visibility?
- What KPI matters most next quarter?
