# Oil & Energy Upstream E&P Research Checklist

Use this checklist when analyzing upstream oil and gas producers.

## Production

- Total BOE/d
- Oil Bbl/d
- Gas MMcf/d
- NGL volumes if material
- QoQ and YoY growth
- Guidance beat or miss

## Realizations and Costs

- Realized oil price
- Realized gas price
- Basin differentials
- LOE
- D&C / well costs
- Cash margin trend

## Capital Efficiency

- Capex versus production growth
- Wells online versus plan
- Lateral length and productivity
- Inventory depth and runway

## Balance Sheet and Returns

- Net debt
- Leverage
- Liquidity
- Dividends
- Buybacks

## Guidance

- Production guidance
- Capex guidance
- Oil mix guidance
- Infrastructure or takeaway milestones

## Sector-Specific Risks

- Oil price drawdown
- Gas differential pressure
- Service cost inflation
- Basin bottlenecks
- Acquisition discipline

## Final Judgment

- Is the company growing efficiently?
- Are results better than guidance because of repeatable execution or one-offs?
- Is the balance sheet improving?
- What metric will prove the thesis next quarter?
