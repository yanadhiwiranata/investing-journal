# Oil & Energy Upstream E&P Research Checklist

Use this checklist when analyzing upstream oil and gas producers. Organize by priority tier for efficient quarterly reviews.

---

## TIER 1: Critical Metrics (Check Every Quarter)

### Production Trajectory
- [ ] Total BOE/d produced (QoQ & YoY trend)
- [ ] Oil Bbl/d (% of total BOE)
- [ ] Natural gas MMcf/d (% of total BOE)
- [ ] NGL volumes (Bbl/d, % of total if material)
- [ ] Production guidance vs. actual (achievement rate)
- [ ] Basin-by-basin production breakdown
  - [ ] Which asset is driving growth or decline?
  - [ ] Any operational disruptions or recoveries?
  - [ ] Conventional vs. unconventional split maintained?
- [ ] YoY production growth: **Benchmark** >2% for majors, >5% for independents
- [ ] Production consistency: **Benchmark** beat or match guidance >80% of time

### Realized Prices & Margins
- [ ] Realized oil price vs. WTI average (basis discount/premium)
- [ ] Realized gas price vs. Henry Hub average (basis discount/premium)
- [ ] NGL realizations if material
- [ ] WTI / Brent environment during quarter
- [ ] Oil margin per Bbl realized: [realized price − LOE − DD&C]
- [ ] Gas margin per Mcf realized: [realized price − LOE − DD&C]
- [ ] Hedging activity: % of production hedged, forward duration, benefit/loss
- [ ] Currency impact if relevant (CAD exposure):
- [ ] Margin stability: **Benchmark** stable or improving; any structural headwinds?
- [ ] Realized margin benchmark: **Benchmark** positive cash margin on oil at $40+ WTI; gas margins positive at $2.50+ Henry Hub

### Unit Costs (LOE, DD&C, Corporate)
- [ ] LOE per BOE (QoQ & YoY)
- [ ] DD&C per BOE (QoQ & YoY)
- [ ] Corporate overhead per BOE
- [ ] Cost bridge: what drove QoQ/YoY change? (labor, service costs, throughput, depreciation)
- [ ] Fixed vs. variable cost split estimate (% of LOE, % of DD&C)
- [ ] Well costs by basin: [$/lateral foot, $/well, trend]
- [ ] Service cost indices: [drilling days/rates, completion pricing, pressure pumping]
- [ ] Energy cost exposure: [electricity, fuel, % of LOE]
- [ ] Labor inflation rate
- [ ] Cost stability: **Benchmark** stable or declining; avoid >10% annual structural inflation
- [ ] LOE per BOE benchmark: **Benchmark** <$8/BOE for majors; <$10/BOE for unconventional independents
- [ ] DD&C per BOE benchmark: **Benchmark** <$8/BOE for low-cost assets; <$12/BOE for developed assets

### Reserve & Inventory Depth
- [ ] Proved reserves (Oil Mbbl + Gas Bcf, total BOE equivalent)
- [ ] Proved + Probable reserves (BOE equivalent)
- [ ] Reserve life index (RLI): Proved reserves / annual production = [X] years
- [ ] Reserve replacement rate: % of annual production replaced = [X]%
- [ ] Reserve per share: BOE per share vs. peer average
- [ ] Inventory runway: [drilling locations at current rate = X years]
- [ ] Reserve revisions: any impairment, downgrade, or upward surprise?
- [ ] Conventional vs. unconventional split (% of reserves)
- [ ] Reserve quality: [location, depth, cost to extract, time to revenue]
- [ ] Reserve benchmark: **Benchmark** RLI >10 years for majors; >15 years for sustainable independents
- [ ] Reserve replacement benchmark: **Benchmark** ≥100% annually; <80% = red flag for mature companies
- [ ] Inventory runway benchmark: **Benchmark** >10–15 years of drilling locations at current capex pace

---

## TIER 2: Financial Health (Check Every Quarter)

### Balance Sheet & Liquidity
- [ ] Cash on hand (absolute amount & in days of operating expense)
- [ ] Total debt outstanding and maturity schedule
- [ ] Debt due in next 12 months vs. expected FCF
- [ ] Undrawn credit facilities and covenant status
- [ ] Net cash / net debt position
- [ ] Annual operating capex burn rate
- [ ] Project capex needs (next 12–24 months, major projects)
- [ ] Total liquidity: [cash + undrawn facilities − near-term debt due]
- [ ] Liquidity cushion: **Benchmark** Total liquidity >3× annual operating capex
- [ ] Debt leverage: **Benchmark** Net debt/EBITDAX <2.0x for majors; <1.5x for independents
- [ ] Debt rating: [investment grade / speculative grade; any downgrade risk?]

### Cash Generation & Capital Efficiency
- [ ] Operating cash flow (OCF) vs. prior quarter
- [ ] Free cash flow (FCF) = OCF − capex (is company self-funding?)
- [ ] FCF conversion: FCF / OCF ratio (capex discipline)
- [ ] Capex intensity: [% of revenue, capex per BOE per day]
- [ ] Return on invested capital (ROIC) on development projects: [NPV / capex at commodity assumption]
- [ ] Dividend paid and coverage (FCF / dividend paid), if applicable
- [ ] Share buyback activity (volume, price paid, authorization status)
- [ ] Capital discipline: [Capex actual vs. budget, large overruns / savings]
- [ ] OCF trend: improving, stable, or declining?
- [ ] FCF benchmark: **Benchmark** Positive FCF; dividend payout ≤50% of FCF for growth companies
- [ ] Capex discipline benchmark: **Benchmark** Capex actual within 5–10% of guidance; major project overruns = red flag

---

## TIER 3: Growth & Execution (Check 2x Annually or Quarterly for Major Projects)

### Development Pipeline & Capex Projects
- [ ] Major projects in development or execution phase (list by name, basin)
- [ ] Expected production start date for each project
- [ ] Capex budget and any overruns / revisions vs. original estimate
- [ ] Drilling plan: wells planned vs. drilled, on-stream pace vs. guidance
- [ ] Well productivity: [type curve BOE/day per well, trend vs. offset wells]
- [ ] Lateral length and stage count trends (improving well economics?)
- [ ] Infrastructure milestones: [pipeline completion, takeaway expansion, processing upgrades]
- [ ] Permitting or lease status: on track / delayed / at risk
- [ ] Environmental or ESG project requirements: [methane capture, flaring reduction]
- [ ] Funding strategy: [internal cash, debt capacity, partner capex]
- [ ] Project execution confidence: on-time / on-budget / on-spec?
- [ ] Execution benchmark: **Benchmark** On-time, on-budget delivery; wells achieving type curve productivity

### Guidance & Outlook
- [ ] Full-year production guidance (updated? raised/lowered? by how much?)
- [ ] LOE guidance (updated? trend direction?)
- [ ] DD&C guidance (updated? trend direction?)
- [ ] Capex guidance for current year & forward outlook (2-year view)
- [ ] Oil mix guidance (% of BOE expected)
- [ ] Project timeline updates (any delays or accelerations?)
- [ ] Hedging plan: [% hedged forward, commodity mix]
- [ ] Management tone on confidence (positive / cautious / concerned)
- [ ] Analyst consensus vs. company guidance (bullish / neutral / bearish spread?)
- [ ] Guidance track record: beat, miss, or match historical average?
- [ ] Guidance benchmark: **Benchmark** Guidance met or slightly beaten; no surprise cuts on production or cost

---

## TIER 4: Risk & External Factors (Check Quarterly + Ad Hoc)

### Commodity Exposure & Market Environment
- [ ] Oil vs. Natural Gas weighting in revenue (% BOE mix)
- [ ] WTI crude price environment (current level, trend vs. cost structure)
- [ ] Henry Hub natural gas price environment (current level, regional basis)
- [ ] Brent-WTI spread or regional price differential
- [ ] Oil price sensitivity: $10 WTI change = $X per share impact
- [ ] Gas price sensitivity: $0.50 Henry Hub change = $X per share impact
- [ ] Price hedging: % of production hedged, forward duration, commodities covered
- [ ] Hedge benchmark: **Benchmark** <50% of production hedged; minimal tail risk to upside

### Jurisdiction, Regulatory & ESG
- [ ] Primary operating jurisdictions: [Country/state, risk tier: low/medium/high]
- [ ] Lease expiration schedule: [material acreage expiring, renewal risk]
- [ ] Royalty & tax burden: [% of revenue by jurisdiction, trend, any rate increases]
- [ ] Recent policy changes: [yes/no; methane regulations, carbon tax, production tax changes]
- [ ] Methane emissions regulation: [regulatory framework, company compliance status, capex requirements, timeline]
- [ ] Carbon pricing or cap-and-trade: [exposure by jurisdiction, cost impact]
- [ ] ESG disclosure requirements: [TCFD, Scope 1-3 emissions tracking, commitment level]
- [ ] Political or geopolitical risk: [sanctions, export controls, operating continuity risk]
- [ ] Currency exposure: [CAD, EUR, other; % of costs and revenue exposure]
- [ ] Local content / partnering requirements: [JV/consortium structure, local ownership %, income obligations]

### Operational & Market Risks
- [ ] Well decline rates: [primary decline curve steepness, impact on production sustainability]
- [ ] Basis or differential pressure: [Henry Hub regional spreads widening? Pipeline take-away full?]
- [ ] Service cost inflation: [drilling index, completion pricing, wage pressure]
- [ ] Basin bottlenecks: [takeaway capacity, processor constraints, midstream M&A impact]
- [ ] Environmental liabilities: [remediation obligations, abandonment cost estimates]
- [ ] Supply chain constraints: [drilling rig availability, equipment lead times, labor availability]
- [ ] Acquisition discipline: [M&A track record, synergy realization, integration success]
- [ ] Operator risk: [third-party operated assets, operator competence, cost control]

## Final Judgment (After Each Earnings)

- [ ] **Production Status:** On track / Ahead / Behind guidance
  - Evidence: [production vs. forecast, revised full-year outlook]

- [ ] **Cost Status:** LOE/DD&C improving / Stable / Deteriorating
  - Evidence: [QoQ/YoY trend, cost bridge, guidance change]

- [ ] **Realization Status:** Receiving commodity price premium / In line / At a discount to benchmarks
  - Evidence: [realized prices vs. WTI/Henry Hub, basis differentials, hedging impact]

- [ ] **Cash Generation:** Strong FCF / Adequate / Pressure
  - Evidence: [FCF absolute amount, yield at current price, capex coverage]

- [ ] **Balance Sheet:** Strong enough for planned capex and returns? Yes / Marginal / Needs capital raise
  - Evidence: [cash position, FCF, debt maturity, project funding needs]

- [ ] **Thesis Validation:** Bull case strengthened / Unchanged / Weakened
  - Evidence: [execution vs. thesis, guidance change, production/cost/price momentum]

- [ ] **Next Critical Data:** What single metric or event matters most next quarter?
  - Answer: [e.g., "production guidance reset", "cost inflation surprise", "reserve replacement rate"]

- [ ] **Recommended Action:** Buy / Hold / Reduce / Sell
  - Rationale: [conviction and time horizon]

---

## Analyst-Specific Priority Weight (Customize as Needed)

**Most Important (80% of conviction):**
- Production growth consistency (YoY rate, guidance beat rate)
- Unit cost control (LOE, DD&C trend; inflation resilience)
- Free cash flow and capital discipline (capex actual vs. budget)

**Secondary (15% of conviction):**
- Reserve replacement and inventory depth
- Realized pricing relative to commodity benchmarks
- Balance sheet strength and debt maturity profile

**Tertiary (5% of conviction):**
- Dividend coverage
- Analyst sentiment shifts
- Geological risk or ESG/regulatory changes
