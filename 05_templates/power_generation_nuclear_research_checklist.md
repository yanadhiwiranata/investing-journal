# Power Generation & Nuclear Research Checklist

Use this checklist for nuclear power operators and clean power generators with AI/data center PPA exposure: CEG, VST, SMR, and similar names.

These are power companies first. The AI angle is real but secondary — the base business (fleet reliability, power prices, capacity payments) must work before the PPA upside matters.

---

## 1. Classify The Business First

- Is this a pure nuclear operator, a mixed nuclear + gas generator, or a speculative SMR developer?
- What % of revenue comes from nuclear vs. gas vs. renewables vs. capacity payments?
- Is the company price-taker (sells at spot) or price-setter (sells on long-term PPA)?
- What % of output is hedged for the next 12 / 24 months, and at what average price?

**Pure-play nuclear (CEG):** Fleet reliability and PPA wins are the story.
**Mixed generator (VST):** Power price environment + capacity markets + nuclear fleet together.
**SMR developer (SMR/NuScale):** Pre-revenue; track regulatory milestones and order backlog instead of financial KPIs.

## 2. Nuclear Fleet Reliability

- What is the nuclear capacity factor this quarter? (90–93% = excellent; <85% = red flag)
- Any unplanned outages? How many days, which plants, what was the lost revenue impact?
- Is the capacity factor improving, stable, or declining vs. prior year?
- What is the refueling outage schedule for the next 12 months? (Planned outages reduce output but are predictable)
- Are there any NRC compliance issues, safety notices, or license conditions outstanding?

## 3. Power Price Environment

- What is the current forward power price curve for the company's primary markets (PJM / ERCOT / MISO)?
- Is the company a net beneficiary of rising power prices (low hedge ratio) or insulated (high hedge ratio)?
- What is the average hedge price vs. current spot? Is there upside if hedges roll off?
- Are capacity market prices rising or falling in PJM / ERCOT? (Capacity payments = stable recurring income)
- Is renewable oversupply or gas price weakness a near-term headwind on spot power prices?

## 4. AI / Data Center PPA Pipeline

- How many MW are currently under signed PPA with data center / hyperscaler customers?
- What is the average $/MWh pricing on signed PPAs? ($80–100/MWh = fair; >$100/MWh = strong)
- What is the contract duration? (Longer = more durable cash flow; shorter = re-pricing risk)
- Are counterparties investment-grade (MSFT, AMZN, GOOGL) or smaller?
- Is there a public pipeline of additional PPA discussions? Any announced but unsigned agreements?
- Has management given MW targets for PPA buildout by year-end or multi-year?

## 5. Regulatory And Policy Checks

- Has the NRC approved or denied any license renewal applications in the last quarter?
- Is the company's oldest fleet plants within 10 years of license expiration? (License renewal = decades of cash flow at stake)
- Is the nuclear production tax credit (IRA PTC, ~$15/MWh) intact or at legislative risk?
- Any DOE loan guarantee, grant, or Inflation Reduction Act benefit that changes the economics?
- For SMR names: What NRC design certification milestones remain? What is the realistic deployment timeline?

## 6. Financial Health

- Is free cash flow positive and growing? (Nuclear is capital-intensive but should be FCF-generative)
- What is the leverage ratio? Nuclear maintenance capex is high — debt must be manageable
- Is the company buying back shares or paying a growing dividend? (Signal of excess cash confidence)
- For SMR: How much cash runway remains before they need additional financing?

## 7. Competitive And Market Position

- Is the company the only nuclear supplier in its grid region? (Structural moat if yes)
- Are competing coal or gas plants retiring, tightening the supply/demand balance in the power market?
- Are new renewable additions outpacing retirements, creating power price pressure?
- Is there a risk of a competing nuclear plant restart (e.g., Palisades) entering the same market?

## 8. Final Judgment

- Is the nuclear fleet running above 90% capacity factor? (Base business working)
- Are power prices supportive in the company's primary market? (Revenue tailwind)
- Is the PPA pipeline growing in size and quality? (Re-rating catalyst)
- Are there near-term NRC or policy risks that could impair the thesis?
- Is the valuation pricing in PPA upside already, or is it still being discounted? (Determines entry urgency)
- What single event would most change the thesis in the next 90 days? (PPA announcement, capacity auction result, NRC decision, earnings capacity factor print)
