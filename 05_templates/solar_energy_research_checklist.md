# Solar Energy Research Checklist

Use this checklist when analyzing solar energy companies.

## Demand and End Markets

- Utility-scale versus commercial versus residential exposure
- New bookings, backlog, or awarded capacity
- Customer concentration
- Geographic demand mix
- Install or shipment growth

## Pricing and Margins

- Module, cell, inverter, tracker, or storage ASP trend
- Gross margin
- Mix shift
- Manufacturing yield or project margin trend
- Input cost and tariff pass-through

## Capacity and Execution

- Nameplate capacity
- Utilization
- Ramp timing versus plan
- Conversion from pipeline to revenue
- Delivery bottlenecks or interconnection delays

## Policy and Trade

- IRA / Section 45X / domestic content exposure
- Tariffs, AD/CVD, or import restrictions
- Tax credit eligibility
- Permitting or grid policy changes

## Balance Sheet

- Cash
- Debt
- Liquidity runway
- Project or plant build capex needs
- Dilution or financing risk

## Final Judgment

- Is demand real or pulled forward by policy timing?
- Are margins improving because of execution or temporary subsidy support?
- Is capacity expansion earning acceptable returns?
- What KPI matters most next quarter?
