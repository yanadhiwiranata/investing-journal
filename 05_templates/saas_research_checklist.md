## Step 1: Company & Market Context

- [ ] **Confirmed:** Company name, ticker, listing exchange, sector (SaaS)
- [ ] **TAM Assessment:** Total addressable market size and growth rate; is TAM expanding or mature?
- [ ] **Business Model:** Subscription / Usage-based / Hybrid; recurring revenue %?
- [ ] **Customer Base:** SMB / Mid-Market / Enterprise; geographic mix
- [ ] **Competitive Position:** Market leader, challenger, niche player; how many direct competitors?
- [ ] **IPO / Funding Status:** When did company go public? Last private valuation if recent IPO?

## Step 2: Financial Data Gathering

### Latest Quarter (Most Recent)
- [ ] **Report Date:** Confirmed YYYY-MM-DD
- [ ] **ARR / MRR:** Absolute value and growth %
- [ ] **Revenue:** Total and growth % (QoQ and YoY)
- [ ] **Gross Margin %:** Trended for 3+ quarters
- [ ] **Operating Income/Loss:** GAAP and adjusted
- [ ] **Free Cash Flow:** Operating cash flow – CapEx
- [ ] **Customer Count:** Total customers and net new customers
- [ ] **NRR (Net Retention Rate):** % or state "not disclosed"
- [ ] **Gross Retention (or Churn):** % or inverse churn

### Prior Quarter (T-1)
- [ ] **Revenue:** Absolute and growth %
- [ ] **Gross Margin %**
- [ ] **ARR/MRR:** Absolute and growth %
- [ ] **Free Cash Flow**
- [ ] **NRR:** If disclosed; track trend

### Same Quarter Prior Year (T-4)
- [ ] **Revenue:** Absolute and growth %
- [ ] **Gross Margin %**
- [ ] **ARR/MRR:** Absolute and growth %
- [ ] **Free Cash Flow**
- [ ] **NRR:** If disclosed; identify seasonal patterns

## Step 3: SaaS Unit Economics Deep Dive

### Revenue & Customer Metrics
- [ ] **ARR Growth Rate (TTM):** ___% (year-over-year)
- [ ] **Net New ARR:** $XXM (absolute or % of prior year ARR)
- [ ] **Customer Count Trend:** Growing / Flat / Declining
- [ ] **Dollar-Based Net Retention (NRR):** ___% or "not disclosed"
  - If >100%: Upsell/cross-sell exceeds churn (health signal)
  - If 95–100%: Churn balanced by expansion
  - If <95%: Net churn = red flag for mature/declining segment
- [ ] **Gross Churn (Customer Count):** ___% (% of customers lost per period)
- [ ] **Net Churn (ARR basis):** ___% or "not disclosed"

### Customer Acquisition Efficiency
- [ ] **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** $XXX or $X,XXX
  - Calculate if not disclosed: Sales & Marketing spend ÷ (Net new ARR ÷ ACV)
- [ ] **CAC Payback Period:** X months (Formula: CAC ÷ Monthly gross profit per customer)
  - Healthy: 12–18 months
  - Excellent: <12 months
  - Concern: >24 months
- [ ] **LTV (Lifetime Value):** $XXX or estimated as (Gross Margin × ACV) ÷ (Monthly Churn %)
- [ ] **LTV/CAC Ratio:** Xx or Xxx
  - Healthy: >3:1
  - Concern: <1.5:1
- [ ] **Rule of 40 (Growth + FCF Margin):** ____%
  - If ≥40: Well-balanced
  - If <40: Either growth slowing or profitability extending

### Pricing & Contract Dynamics
- [ ] **Average Contract Value (ACV):** $XXX or $X,XXX
- [ ] **Contract Duration:** Annual / Multi-year / Mix
- [ ] **Pricing Tiers:** Self-serve / SMB / Mid / Enterprise; price points
- [ ] **ACV Trend:** Growing (upsell/expansion), flat, or declining
- [ ] **Expansion Revenue (% of total):** Upsell/cross-sell/seat expansion as % of new bookings
