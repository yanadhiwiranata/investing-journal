## 6. Indonesia Banking Sector — Cross-Bank Impact Matrix

| Factor | BBCA | BBRI | BMRI |
|--------|------|------|------|
| **BI Rate hike** | Mild headwind — low CoF CASA fortress; slight NIM benefit on asset repricing | Significant headwind — micro/UMKM borrowers rate-sensitive; CoF rises | Moderate — corporate book reprices; NIM squeeze on cost side |
| **IDR weakness** | Low direct — minimal FCY loans; indirect via higher-for-longer BI rates | Moderate — KUR in IDR; mainly indirect via slowing economy | High — large corporate FCY component; commodity clients exposed |
| **UMKM NPL cycle** | Very low — minimal UMKM book | Very high — ~80% of loans; reclassification risk post-OJK window | Low — ~10% micro/KUR |
| **KUR quota expansion** | None | Primary — BBRI ~60% market share | Moderate |
| **Infrastructure spending / IKN** | Minimal | Low | High — primary infrastructure lender |
| **OJK BNPL rules** | Moderate — digital BNPL | Low | High — Livin' app BNPL book |
| **BI rate cut cycle begins** | Strong positive — P/BV re-rating first | Strong positive — CoF falls faster; NIM expands | Positive — corporate NIM + credit activity |
| **BUMN dividend mandate** | Not applicable (private) | Limits book value growth | Same as BBRI |

## 8. Key Definitions & Abbreviations

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **BI** | Bank Indonesia — Indonesia's central bank |
| **OJK** | Otoritas Jasa Keuangan — financial services regulator (banks, insurance, capital markets) |
| **BPS** | Badan Pusat Statistik — national statistics agency |
| **BUMN** | Badan Usaha Milik Negara — State-Owned Enterprise |
| **UMKM** | Usaha Mikro, Kecil, dan Menengah — micro, small, and medium enterprises |
| **KUR** | Kredit Usaha Rakyat — government-subsidized UMKM loans |
| **GWM** | Giro Wajib Minimum — mandatory reserve requirement held at BI |
| **7DRRR** | 7-Day Reverse Repo Rate — BI's benchmark policy rate |
| **DSIB / D-SIB** | Domestic Systemically Important Bank — extra capital buffer requirement |
| **BOPO** | Beban Operasional / Pendapatan Operasional — Indonesian cost-to-income ratio |
| **LFR** | Loan-to-Funding Ratio — OJK liquidity metric |
| **NIM** | Net Interest Margin |
| **CoF** | Cost of Funds |
| **CASA** | Current Account + Savings Account — low-cost deposits |
| **IDX** | Indonesia Stock Exchange (Bursa Efek Indonesia) |
| **IKN** | Ibu Kota Negara — new capital city project (East Kalimantan) |
| **PLM** | Penyangga Likuiditas Makroprudensial — macroprudential liquidity buffer (SBN) |
| **DNDF** | Domestic Non-Deliverable Forward — BI FX hedging tool |
