# Automotive Research Checklist

Use this checklist when analyzing automotive names. Adapt depth based on company stage, geography, and urgency.

---

## Pre-Analysis Setup

- [ ] Confirm analysis date and current market date
- [ ] Verify latest reported quarter from company IR site or SEC filing
- [ ] Check next earnings date and any guidance changes since last report
- [ ] Identify company subsector: [OEM / EV Pure-Play / Legacy Incumbent / Supplier / Chinese EV]
- [ ] Identify primary geography: [China / USA / Europe / Multi-region]
- [ ] Note if China EV name — plan to use CnEVPost and CarNewsChina for data

---

## Operating Performance (Last 3 Quarters)

### Volume & Deliveries

- [ ] Unit deliveries or sales for current quarter and prior two quarters
- [ ] QoQ growth rate and YoY growth rate
- [ ] Monthly delivery trend for China EV names from CnEVPost (last 6 months minimum)
- [ ] Model-by-model sales breakdown for China EV names (identify hero models vs. portfolio depth)
- [ ] Retail vs. fleet mix (if applicable)
- [ ] Regional delivery split (China %, USA %, Europe %, other %)
- [ ] Backlog or order trend (is demand healthy or slowing?)
- [ ] Industry context: is the company gaining or losing market share?

### Pricing & Mix

- [ ] Average selling price (ASP) trend QoQ and YoY
- [ ] Gross margin trend (identify mix contribution vs. cost contribution)
- [ ] Product mix shift (EV % of volume, premium % of volume)
- [ ] Incentive and discount intensity (is discounting widening or narrowing?)
- [ ] ASP by model for China EV names (premium positioning, competitive pricing)
- [ ] Currency headwinds / tailwinds if exporter (FX impact on reported ASP)
- [ ] Pricing power vs. competitors (ability to raise prices or forced to discount)

### Profitability & Margins

- [ ] Gross margin % and trend (QoQ, YoY, and by region if available)
- [ ] EV gross margin vs. ICE gross margin (is EV still a drag?)
- [ ] Operating margin % and trend
- [ ] Fixed-cost absorption rate (are plants running at capacity or underutilized?)
- [ ] Variable cost per unit trend (inflation or deflation?)
- [ ] Financing income / contribution (if relevant to reported profits)

### Cash Flow & Liquidity

- [ ] Operating cash flow (QoQ, YoY comparison)
- [ ] Free cash flow (OCF - capex)
- [ ] Working capital changes (inventory, receivables, payables trends)
- [ ] Inventory days outstanding (is inventory building up?)
- [ ] Dealer inventory levels if available (growing concern or normalizing?)
- [ ] Capital expenditure (% of revenue, capex guidance forward)

---

## Financial Quality

### Revenue & Growth

- [ ] Total revenue trend (QoQ and YoY)
- [ ] Revenue by geographic segment (China, USA, Europe, other)
- [ ] Revenue growth rate forecasted for current year
- [ ] Guidance raised or lowered from prior quarter (and reason)
- [ ] Mix contribution to growth (volume vs. ASP vs. product mix)

### Profitability Trend

- [ ] Net income trend (QoQ, YoY)
- [ ] Net margin trend
- [ ] EV profitability status (when does EV division turn profitable?)
- [ ] One-time charges or items affecting comparability

### Balance Sheet & Debt

- [ ] Total debt, net debt, and leverage ratio
- [ ] Debt maturity schedule (any refinancing risk near-term?)
- [ ] Cash position and liquidity
- [ ] Pension / post-retirement obligations
- [ ] Any covenant or covenant waiver issues

### Capital Allocation

- [ ] Capex intensity (% of revenue) and guidance
- [ ] R&D spending (% of revenue) — especially for EV/autonomy development
- [ ] Dividend policy and sustainability
- [ ] Share buyback program status
- [ ] M&A or strategic investments

---

## EV Transition & Product Strategy

### EV Mix & Profitability

- [ ] EV as % of total volume (current and trend)
- [ ] EV as % of total revenue (current and trend)
- [ ] Gross margin on EV products vs. ICE products
- [ ] When does EV division or product reach profitability?
- [ ] EV pricing strategy (premium, mass-market, or mixed?)

### Technology & Competitive Positioning

- [ ] Battery sourcing strategy (captive, JV, third-party)
- [ ] Battery cost per kWh and trend (improving or deteriorating?)
- [ ] Semiconductor / chip sourcing and availability (still a bottleneck?)
- [ ] Software and autonomy capability (Lidar, self-driving features, OTA updates)
- [ ] Partnerships with tech companies (Tesla, BYD, Nvidia, others)
- [ ] IP or patent defensibility

### Product Roadmap

- [ ] New EV model launches planned (next 12-24 months)
- [ ] Refresh or facelift timing for current models
- [ ] For China EV names: upcoming launches from CarNewsChina
- [ ] Model segmentation strategy (entry-level, mid-tier, premium)
- [ ] Expected volumes and ASP for new launches
- [ ] Time-to-market advantage or disadvantage vs. competitors

---

## Supply Chain & Cost Structure

### Input Costs

- [ ] Battery cost trends (if battery is a major P&L driver)
- [ ] Commodity exposure (steel, aluminum, critical minerals)
- [ ] Semiconductor availability and lead times
- [ ] Energy costs (electricity for manufacturing, fuel for logistics)
- [ ] Labor cost inflation by geography
- [ ] Any cost hedging programs in place

### Manufacturing Efficiency

- [ ] Plant utilization rate (is capacity underused?)
- [ ] Production ramp progress for new models
- [ ] Automation and capex to improve efficiency
- [ ] Warranty and recall costs (any red flags?)
- [ ] Supply chain disruption risk (geopolitical, weather, port congestion)

### Structural Cost Position

- [ ] Fixed vs. variable cost split (high fixed costs = operating leverage or risk?)
- [ ] Labor cost per unit vs. competitors
- [ ] Manufacturing footprint (where are plants? tariff exposure?)
- [ ] Outsourcing strategy (build or partner for EV components?)

---

## Regional & Geographic Analysis

### China Market (if applicable)

- [ ] EV vs. ICE mix in China (penetration rate, trend)
- [ ] Pricing trends in China (is competition intensifying?)
- [ ] Chinese competitor threat assessment (BYD, NIO, XPeng, Li Auto, others)
- [ ] Tariff exposure if exporting from China
- [ ] Government incentives for EV still in effect?
- [ ] Monthly delivery tracking from CnEVPost for all models
- [ ] Product competitiveness vs. local EV makers (technology, features, price)

### USA Market (if applicable)

- [ ] EV penetration rate and trend
- [ ] IRA (Inflation Reduction Act) benefit status (if applicable)
- [ ] Tariff exposure on Chinese EV imports or Chinese-made components
- [ ] Dealer network strength
- [ ] Inventory levels in dealer network
- [ ] Competitive position vs. Tesla and other EV makers

### Europe Market (if applicable)

- [ ] Emission regulations and compliance pathway
- [ ] EV incentives status in major markets (Germany, UK, France, others)
- [ ] Tariff or trade barriers
- [ ] Competitor activity
- [ ] Demand trends

### Currency Headwinds / Tailwinds

- [ ] If exporter, major currency pairs affecting reported results
- [ ] FX impact on earnings (if significant, note magnitude)
- [ ] Any hedging programs?

---

## Competitive Landscape

### Peer Comparison

- [ ] List 3-5 key competitors and their current market position
- [ ] Compare gross margins, operating margins, EV penetration
- [ ] Compare EV roadmap maturity and launch timing
- [ ] Cost competitiveness (capex intensity, labor costs)
- [ ] Brand strength and pricing power by company
- [ ] Technology differentiation (autonomous, software, battery)

### Market Share Trends

- [ ] Company's market share in key segments (total, premium, EV)
- [ ] Gaining or losing share vs. peers?
- [ ] Any segment vulnerability to new entrants or stronger competitors?

---

## Guidance & Forward Outlook

### Quarterly Guidance Track Record

- [ ] How often has management beaten or missed guidance?
- [ ] Any pattern of conservative or aggressive guidance?
- [ ] Guidance for current year: volumes, margins, capex, FCF

### Forward Guidance

- [ ] Management's volume guidance for next 12-24 months
- [ ] Margin guidance and pathway to improvement/compression
- [ ] Capex guidance and strategic investment priorities
- [ ] EV volume targets and profitability inflection timing
- [ ] Any changes to prior guidance (raised/lowered/withdrawn)?

### Analyst Consensus (TipRanks, Bloomberg)

- [ ] Most recent analyst ratings (last 5 ratings)
- [ ] Consensus price target and range
- [ ] Current stock price vs. consensus target (upside/downside %)
- [ ] Rating momentum (are analysts upgrading or downgrading?)
- [ ] Key difference between bull and bear cases in analyst research

---

## Macro & Industry Cycle Context

### Auto Industry Cycle Position

- [ ] Where is the industry in the cycle? (Early recovery, peak, downturn)
- [ ] Global new vehicle sales trend (growing or declining?)
- [ ] Credit availability and consumer health (important for demand)
- [ ] Inventory cycle position (are inventories at healthy, elevated, or depleted levels?)
- [ ] Incentive intensity across the industry (is discounting accelerating?)

### Commodity & Material Price Impact

- [ ] Impact of steel prices on COGS
- [ ] Impact of battery/lithium prices (if relevant)
- [ ] Impact of semiconductor prices (if supply-constrained)
- [ ] Is the company exposed to commodity price movement? (Sensitivity estimate)

### Trade & Tariff Environment

- [ ] USA tariff risk (on imports, on Chinese-made components)
- [ ] EU tariff or trade barriers
- [ ] Any pending tariff changes that could impact margin?

---

## Pre-Earnings Analysis (if applicable)

### What To Expect

- [ ] Analyst consensus for revenue and EPS
- [ ] Key metrics to watch (units, ASP, margins, FCF)
- [ ] What surprised last quarter (beat/miss magnitude)?
- [ ] What are the street's key questions going into earnings?

### Prediction & Scenario

- [ ] **Pre-earnings prediction:** Will the company beat or miss guidance? By how much?
- [ ] **Key driver:** Which single metric is most critical (units? margins? margin guidance?)
- [ ] **Bull scenario:** What news would drive the stock higher post-earnings?
- [ ] **Bear scenario:** What news would trigger a sell-off?
- [ ] **Base scenario:** Company executes at expectations, stock reaction?

### Price Reaction Context

- [ ] What is already priced into the stock (high expectations / low expectations)?
- [ ] How much upside/downside is implied by current valuation vs. bull/base/bear cases?
- [ ] Is the risk/reward attractive before earnings?

---

## Final Judgment Checklist

- [ ] **Demand health:** Is demand organic or being bought with incentives? Is backlog solid?
- [ ] **Margin trajectory:** Are margins improving, stable, or compressing? Structural or cyclical?
- [ ] **EV execution:** Is the EV transition on track? Is EV profitable yet or when?
- [ ] **Competition:** Is the company gaining share or losing to peers/new entrants?
- [ ] **For China EV names:** Is growth sustained across multiple models or dependent on one hero model? Is the company competitive on price/features vs. BYD and other Chinese EV makers?
- [ ] **Cost structure:** Is the company well-positioned on costs or facing headwinds?
- [ ] **Capital allocation:** Is management deploying capital wisely (capex, dividend, buyback)?
- [ ] **Valuation:** Is the stock trading at a discount, fair value, or premium to intrinsic value?
- [ ] **Key KPI next quarter:** What single metric will decide the thesis for the next quarter?
- [ ] **Conviction and next review:** High / Medium / Low conviction. When to review next?

---

## Notes for China EV Specific Analysis

When analyzing Chinese EV companies (NIO, XPeng, Li Auto, BYD, Geely, others):

- [ ] **Monthly delivery tracking is critical.** Use CnEVPost monthly data (minimum 6 months).
- [ ] **Model-level understanding is essential.** Which models are driving growth? Are new launches succeeding or struggling?
- [ ] **CarNewsChina product updates are vital.** Track facelifts, new model announcements, pricing changes monthly.
- [ ] **Competitive pricing pressure is intense.** BYD, Nio, XPeng, Li Auto, Geely, GAC Aion are all competing on price and features. Where does your company stand?
- [ ] **Export momentum is new.** Some Chinese EV makers are exporting to Southeast Asia, Europe. Is this a margin opportunity or a volume necessity?
- [ ] **Government subsidies may be declining.** Track when new energy vehicle (NEV) incentives are scheduled to phase out.
- [ ] **Technology and features matter.** Battery swapping (NIO), autonomous driving (XPeng), smart EV positioning — these differentiate.
- [ ] **Supply chain for batteries is critical.** Who supplies batteries? CATL, BYD Batteries, LFP vs. NCM? Cost and supply security matter.
