# Automotive Research Checklist

Use this checklist when analyzing automotive names.

## Demand

- Deliveries or shipments
- Monthly delivery trend for China EV names from CnEVPost
- Model-by-model sales breakdown for China EV names when available
- New model launches, facelifts, and price changes for China EV names from CarNewsChina
- Retail versus fleet mix
- Regional demand
- Backlog or order trend

## Pricing

- ASP trend
- Incentives
- Mix shift
- Commodity or tariff pass-through

## Margins

- Gross margin
- EBIT margin
- Fixed-cost absorption
- Financing contribution if relevant

## Operations

- Production ramp
- Inventory levels
- Dealer channel health
- Supply chain bottlenecks

## Strategy

- EV roadmap
- New model cycle
- Product refresh cadence and launch timing for China EV names
- Software or autonomy progress if relevant
- Capital allocation

## Final Judgment

- Is demand healthy or being bought with incentives?
- For China EV names, is growth sustained across multiple models or dependent on one hero model?
- Are margins cyclical or structurally improving?
- If earnings are upcoming, what is the pre-earnings prediction and what short-term price reaction is already priced in?
- Is inventory building a future problem?
- What KPI will decide the thesis next quarter?
